Crab Nebula Lookbook

The shattered remnant of a star the Crab Nebula is a beautiful hot spot of the Universe and for all of us in the Milky Way galaxy to observe and enjoy.

The galaxy within

The Crab Nebula lies within the Milky Way galaxy, more specifically within Perseus's arm. For more info check out Wiki_Crab

What's Nearby?

At 6,500 ly (light years) away Earth is a Neighbor to the Crab Nebula.

The First Identification

The Nebula was first recorded and documented by Lord Rosse in 1844.

  • Supernova recorded by Chinese astronomers in 1054
  • Observed by Charles Messier in 1757
  • Observed many times by Fredrick William Hershel between 1773 and 1809.

Observe

The Crab Pulsar was found within the Crab Nebula in the early 1960's they used advanced telescopes such as the hubble telescope to observe it's deep green hue and light wavelengths.

What is a nebula?

Simply put a Nebula is a giant cloud of gas and dust in space. This is a loose definition dust here encompasses more than it would for earth materia.
